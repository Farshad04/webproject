Real Madrid told 23-year-old is ahead of Kylian Mbappe

Real Madrid are on the verge of winning another historic double. The La Liga title is already in the bag and they got there pretty comfortably. On the other hand, they are also in the Champions League final, where they will taking on Borussia Dortmund. Yet, Los Blancos could potentially sign the best player in the world to get even stronger. That player is none other than Kylian Mbappe. The Frenchman has already announced that he will be leaving PSG at the end of the season. Where will he go? Well, it would be a major surprise if the answer to that question does not turn out to be Real Madrid. For years, there have been links and it looks like the move is finally set to happen this summer. Do Real Madrid really need him though? Having every great player in the world is not always the key to winning. We saw that with the Galacticos. And we saw that at PSG as well, who had Mbappe, Neymar and Lionel Messi at the same time but could not come close to challenging for the Champions League. While Mbappe is a global superstar and a World Cup winner, Vinicius Junior is making a case for being the best player in the world as well.
