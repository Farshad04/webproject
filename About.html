Our Story

We are two students from Tishk International University, studying in the IT department. We created this website as a project for our web design class taught by D. Rebin Mohammed. We are currently in the second grade of our program.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a user-friendly and informative website that serves as a platform to showcase our skills in web design and development. We strive to deliver high-quality content and engaging experiences to our visitors. Through this project, we aim to demonstrate our capabilities, learn new techniques, and contribute positively to our academic and professional growth. As IT students at Tishk International University, our mission is to leverage our technical skills and knowledge to create an innovative and impactful website. We are committed to applying our expertise in web design and development to produce a platform that not only showcases our abilities but also serves as a valuable resource for our audience. Through this project, we aim to demonstrate the practical application of our education and contribute meaningfully to the field of technology.
